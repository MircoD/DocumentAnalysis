The data file contains reviews of Amazon products, grouped by product id.
The reviews are about DvD's. 
Every review has 7 categories, userId, profileName, helpfulness of the review, score(from 1 to 5), time of the review, summary of review and the review itself.
There are 183389 reviews. 
There are no reviews of HP DH Part 2, but reviews of every other HP so the data probably was taken between release dates of HP DH 1 and HP DH 2.
